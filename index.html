ABOUT ME

Fashion is not just style — it is transformation. I am Hamed, a fashion creative based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, with a deep passion for building identities through clothing, texture, and visual storytelling.

My work explores the tension between tradition and modernity, softness and edge, silence and statement. Every look I curate is a chapter in a larger narrative — one that challenges convention and celebrates individuality.

Concept & Story

01

The Philosophy

Every garment carries memory. Every silhouette holds a story. My creative process begins not with fabric, but with feeling — a mood, a tension, an unspoken truth that demands to be worn.

02

The Process

From mood board to final look, I work in chapters. Each collection is built around a single emotional core — then pushed until the idea breaks into something unexpected and real.

03

The Vision

I believe fashion is the most democratic art form — wearable, immediate, personal. My goal is to create looks that don't just photograph well, but that transform the person wearing them.
